Example Thesis

“Quantifying Latinx immigrants through data and biotechnology is a means of further control—to quantify an undocumented person invites the threat of no data or incorrect data (for example, undocumented people who use false social security numbers). Quantifying immigrants also allows the government to put those people back in their ordered place; removing them from the unpredictability of borderlands, markets, and industries in the United States.” (Villa-Nicholas, 2023)
