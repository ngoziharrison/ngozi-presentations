Two sides to the hesitance of Sociology Hesitant

Du Bois describes the hesitance of positivist sociology to study the dynamism and irreducible life of people in the pursuit of laws about society

Du Bois also displays his hesitance to fully accept the formal abstractions, problem framing, laws, and the epistemological foundation of euro-modernity within his scientific practice
