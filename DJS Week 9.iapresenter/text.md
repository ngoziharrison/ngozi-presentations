# What makes a good answer
	- Define your terms
	- Cite examples from course lectures, discussions, and readings
	- Your discussion of the issue should reflect an enagement with the themes of the class. I should be able to understand that you not only attended class but have internalized and can creatively apply course themes and concepts
	- Don't stop at defining concepts, instead provide an analysis of an issue, sythesize information, use concepts to make an argument
