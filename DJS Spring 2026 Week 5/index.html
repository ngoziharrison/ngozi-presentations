A critical engagement with the notion of theory free inference

8

What is the connection between the theory-free ideal and the value-free ideal?

Theory-free ideal

ideal or paradigm that the data will speak for itself free of the specific theory that informs the application of scientific methods

Value-free ideal

Ideal of paradigm that values should not and/or do not affect scientific reasoning

9

Values encoded in machine learning research

“We apply our scheme to annotate 100 influential ML research papers and extract their value commitments, including identifying 59 values significant in machine learning research. These papers reflect and shape the values of the field.” (Birhane et al., 2022)

Developing your writing through reading

How to improve writing through reading

There are multiple ways to engage with reading. One way is reading for content, another is reading for style, structure, and form. In this activity we will be reading for the latter.

13

Find authors you enjoy reading

  • Look for style, clarity, structure
  • Find model texts from these authors and supplement them with writing from your discipline
  • Learn a commonly used citation style in your discipline
14

Common questions that be addressed by looking at model texts

  • How should I frame my argument?
  • What is a good example of a thesis statement?
  • How can I use this piece of evidence to support my argument?
  • How can I introduce a citation or quote?
