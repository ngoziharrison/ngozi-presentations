Du Bois' Critique

In this short article, Du Bois critiques positivist sociology and expresses a skepticism about the application of abstractions in sociological work

I argue that this hesitence is increasingly relevant to work within machine learning and any pursuit of fairness, ethics, transparency

Two sides to the hesitence of Sociology Hesitant

Du Bois describes the hesitance of positivist sociology to study the dynamism and irreducible life of people in the pursuit of laws about society

Du Bois also displays his hesitance to fully accept the formal abstractions, laws, and the epistemological foundation of euro-modernity within his scientific practice
